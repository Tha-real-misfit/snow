Do you dream of experiencing pure freedom?
Well then welcome to snowboarding.
A pastime which involves grabbing a lift to the top of a mountain, strapping in, and hitting the terrain!
Snowboarding is the process of sliding down a hill of snow whilst being locked into a board by your feet.
Snowboarding evolved from the combinations of surfing and skateboarding. It contains the side stance of a skateboarder and the quick turns of a surfer.
Snowboarding can occur all throughout the world at different stages of the year, depending where you are located. Here in Australia we have sixteen ski resorts in total, although only five really feed the needs of serious boarders.
Snowboarding can be for anyone and everyone no matter age or fitness level, there is room for all on the mountain.
Snowboarding can be done as an individual or in a group setting, involving friends and family. What a great way to spend time outdoors in nature.
There are different aspects which make up snowboarding, including freeriding, racing and tricks. Each offering a different level of gratification.
Snowboarding creates a feeling of being free, flying down a big wide-open space as fast as you can go, there is nothing like it!
There is a set of recommendations for snowboarders, these include, beginners should take lessons with qualified instructors, look where you want to go, wear appropriate clothing, including protective equipment.

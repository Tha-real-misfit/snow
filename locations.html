Where should I go?


If you are willing to travel, the options are endless.
Japan is known for having the best powder in the world, whilst New Zealand has some of the best scenery you will ever see.

NEW ZEALAND

New Zealand has a total of eighteen resorts spreading throughout the country. Flying into Queenstown will offer one of the most picturesque scenes available. The top four major resorts include,

  • CARDRONA
  • TREBLE CONE
  • REMARKABLES
  • CORONET PEAK
  • Mount Hutt

AUSTRALIA

The Australian snow season generally runs from June through to August. With resorts located on the east coast including states New South Wales, Victoria, and Tasmania. Popular resorts include,

  • FALLS CREEK, VIC
  • MOUNT BULLER, VIC
  • MOUNT HOTHAM, VIC
  • PERISHER, NSW
  • THREDBO, NSW

JAPAN

Japan hosts an amazing twelve different ski resorts in the country. Hosting possibly the best powder in the world, japans ski resorts have become popular. A few of the top spots to check out in Japan include,

  • MYOKO KOGEN
  • HAKUBA
  • RUSUTSU
  • NISEKO
  • SHIGA KOGEN
